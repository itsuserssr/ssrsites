Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

>>How do I book a cab?

Fill out the form with your pickup and drop location, select the cab type, and click "Book Now".

>>Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 1 hour before the scheduled journey time.

>>What payment options do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and cash payments.

>>What kind of cabs we can book?

You can book airport cabs and intercity cabs, outstation cabs for both one-way transfers & round-trip transfers.

>>What are the advantages of online taxi booking?

Online taxi booking not only helps you with best prices but also helps you with the convenience of paying through multiple payment options with it's friendly pricing. You can easily compare prices and choose the suitable one.
